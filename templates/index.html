Hello there, and thank you for deciding to take this survey!

In this survey, you will be shown various graphs that visualize ten data points.
Two of these points will be marked.
You will be prompted to input what percentage the smaller marked value is from the bigger.
For example, write down 35 if you think the smaller section represents 35% of the value of the bigger one.

In the charts that contain both black and white sections, the data is represented by the black section.
Once you are sure of your answer, press Next.

You are able to go back any time to change your answer, until you press Finish on the final question.

** please take survey in fullscreen on a PC or laptop
